About

Pantaleon Consulting supports research teams and educational programs to practice accountable, community-centred research. My work integrates Indigenous epistemologies, land-based approaches, and participatory action research to create meaningful, sustained impact.

Services

  • PAR & Action Research project design — Collaborative protocols, co-creation, shared governance, and ethical agreements.
  • Methodological advising — Culturally-grounded methods, translanguaging approaches, and mixed-methods integration.
  • Curriculum & Workshop design — Land-based modules, transdisciplinary STEM pedagogy, and community-driven learning.
  • Evaluation & impact planning — Formative and summative evaluation that honours community criteria of success.

Selected projects

  • Land-based curriculum development — Partnered with Indigenous community educators to co-design a seasonal, place-based curriculum.
  • STEM & Computer Coding with youth storytellers — Youth-led photographic and oral history project connecting language revival and local STEM inquiry.
  • Critical quantitative reasoning — Graduate seminar series integrating statistical literacy with decolonial critique.
