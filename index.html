About Umar (Keoni) Pantaleon Umangay, PhD,OCT

I am a scholar-practitioner in sociology of education and Indigenous education. My work focuses on decolonizing research, teacher education, curriculum design, and community-engaged learning. I have taught at the post-secondary level, contributed to research and curriculum projects across educational settings, and published widely on Indigenous knowledge, anti-colonial praxis, and reflective professional practice.

I offer consulting, workshops, and public scholarship for organizations seeking research-informed and culturally grounded approaches to education.
